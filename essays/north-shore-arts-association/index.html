The North Shore Arts Association

May 31, 2026

by Janet Knott

Nothing is more iconic in the art world than the North Shore Arts Association. Founded in 1922 it has been a place for summer shows, auctions, artist demonstrations and talks. Certainly, this summer it will continue in the tradition! Bringing glorious art work to visitors, and Gloucester residents alike!
